Why Chia Seeds?

Loaded with Antioxidants

Antioxidants are compounds that fight free radicals, promote tissue repair and protect against skin damage. Chia seeds have been proven to contain more antioxidants than blueberries!

High in Omega-3 Fatty Acids

Omega-3 fatty acids EPA and DHA are critical for normal brain function and development throughout all stages of life. Chia seeds contain more omega-3's than salmon, gram for gram.

High in Quality Protein

Chia seeds contain about 14% protein, which is very high compared to most plants. Chia seeds also have a good balance of essential amino acids so your body can make the most use of the protein.
